# 01. Project Introduction & Overview

## 1.1 Executive Summary
**Ruqi Store** is a specialized, single-vendor e-commerce web application engineered specifically for the furniture retail sector. The platform acts as a digital bridge, seamlessly connecting a physical furniture showroom with a modern, high-performance digital shopping experience. 

## 1.2 Problem Statement & Solution
Traditional furniture showrooms are restricted by geographical boundaries and physical floor space. Customers often struggle to browse full catalogs or visualize inventory availability. 

**Ruqi Store solves this by providing:**
* A complete, persistent online showcase of all available furniture items.
* Direct digital transaction processing for standard retail buyers.
* A hybrid operational model supporting physical showroom appointments specifically designed for high-ticket buyers who require physical evaluation before making major financial commitments.

## 1.3 Core Project Parameters
* **Domain Focus:** Restricted entirely to high-quality furniture items.
* **Vendor Structure:** Single-vendor system (No multi-vendor marketplace complexities).
* **Target Audience:** Digital retail furniture shoppers and VIP showroom appointment clients.
